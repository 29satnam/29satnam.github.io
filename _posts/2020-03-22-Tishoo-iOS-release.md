## How does it work and what does it do??

Tishoo is a simple yet advance app that tells you how much toilet paper you need.

With this easy to use calculator you can figure out how long your toilet paper stock will last and how much you really need to buy.


With just a few inputs such as number of rolls, number of members using the toilets, number of visits and your toilet paper usage style, you can accurately figure out how many days your toilet paper stock will last.
